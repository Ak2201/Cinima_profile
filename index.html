01 About

Some people find cinema. Cinema found me. I am ArunKumar Mohanan — an actor and director who believes that the most powerful stories are the ones that leave you breathless and changed.

Trained in both acting and direction at the prestigious LV Prasad Film Institute, I bring a dual perspective to every project — understanding the craft from both sides of the lens. My debut short film "Expectation" reached over 5.7 million viewers and was selected for the Chennai Short Film Festival, proving that authentic stories find their audience.

Rooted in South Indian cinema's rich tradition of emotional storytelling, I chase characters that live in the grey — conflicted, raw, and deeply human. Whether performing or directing, my purpose remains the same: to make the audience feel something they cannot forget.

"Cinema is not what you see. It is what you feel long after the screen goes dark."
